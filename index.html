Trek inside USS Enterprise-D

These photographs were taken by Albert Davidson during a special trek inside the set of Star Trek: The Next Generation. Gregory Davidson invited friends and family to visit the set of Star Trek: The Next Generation through Make-A-Wish, while fighting thyroid cancer as a teen. Thanks to the help of the cast, crew, and Make-A-Wish Wish Granters, his wish to see the Enterprise-D became an unforgettable, cherished memory.

This website is curated by Ethan Davidson, son of Albert Davidson and nephew of Gregory Davidson.
