Trek inside USS Enterprise-D

These photographs were taken in 1992 by Albert Davidson during a special trek inside the set of Star Trek: The Next Generation. Gregory Davidson invited family and friends to visit the set of Star Trek: The Next Generation through Make-A-Wish, while fighting thyroid cancer as a teen. Thanks to the help of the cast, crew, and Make-A-Wish Wish Granters, his wish to see the Enterprise-D became an extraordinary reality. Gregory eventually defeated this bout with cancer and was able to continue living his life.

These photographs were scanned and curated in 2025 by Ethan Davidson, son of Albert Davidson and nephew of Gregory Davidson.
